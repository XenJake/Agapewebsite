Our Faculty

At Agape Academy International, our faculty is the heart of our mission. Each member is dedicated to nurturing students academically, spiritually, and emotionally, ensuring they thrive both in their studies and in their faith. Our educators are not only skilled in their respective fields but also deeply committed to Christian values, creating a warm and supportive learning environment where students can grow in character and knowledge.
