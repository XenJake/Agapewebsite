Welcome to Agape Academy International,where Faith, Excellence, and Compassion Grow Together.

At Agape Academy International, we believe that education is more than just academics—it’s an opportunity to shape lives through a commitment to faith, excellence, and service. We are a Christ-centered learning community dedicated to developing students who excel academically, grow spiritually, and serve others with a heart of compassion.



Why Agape Academy? Agape, which means "unconditional love" in Greek, defines our school’s foundation. From preschool through high school, we offer a nurturing environment where students are valued and supported, encouraged to explore their unique talents, and inspired to achieve their God-given potential. Our name reflects our core mission: to model Christ’s love and to empower young minds to embrace lifelong learning and leadership.



We offer a balanced curriculum that integrates rigorous academics with biblical principles. From core subjects to arts, sports, and community service, every part of our program is designed to provide a well-rounded education that prepares students to thrive in college, career, and life.

Our classes and activities are grounded in biblical teachings that help students grow in their personal faith. We provide a challenging curriculum with dedicated faculty, advanced courses, and individualized learning support.

Character and Leadership. Through service projects, student-led initiatives, and a strong emphasis on character, we help students develop leadership skills and a heart for others.


We invite you to discover the difference an Agape education can make. Here, your child will be part of a loving community that values each student’s potential and encourages them to shine brightly for God. Explore our campus, meet our dedicated faculty, and see firsthand how our programs foster growth in every area of a child’s life.



Together, let’s inspire a love for learning, a passion for faith, and a commitment to service. Welcome to Agape Academy International—where your journey to a brighter future begins.
